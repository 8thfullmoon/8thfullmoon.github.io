Travel Arrangements

Air Travel

The closest commercial airport is in Wilmington, North Carolina (ILM). It features routes to and from Washington-Dulles on United; Charlotte and Dallas on American; and Atlanta on Delta. Secondarily, Myrtle Beach, SC (MYR) is also within reasonable driving proximity.

Please plan to rent a car. There are not public transportation options, and car hire services like Uber/Lyft do not reliably operate in the area.

Lodging Options

The best positioned hotel option is the Holiday Inn Express & Suites near Southport, NC. It is a short drive to the venue as well as to the Nicolaisen family home.

The closest Marriott-branded hotel is the Fairfield Inn in Southport, and the closest Hilton-branded hotel is the Hampton Inn & Suites near Southport.

There are a number of beautiful boutique hotels and AirBnb options in Southport and Oak Island as well.
